An AI-powered website auditing platform that analyzes how optimized a website is for AI systems, semantic search engines, and Large Language Models (LLMs).

The platform evaluates AI discoverability, semantic SEO structure, metadata quality, crawlability, and AI answer extraction readiness through a modern interactive analytics dashboard.

# 🧠 Problem Statement

Modern AI systems and Large Language Models (LLMs) do not interpret websites the same way humans do.

A website may look visually polished to users while still being difficult for AI systems to understand, crawl, or extract meaningful information from.

This project helps identify structural and semantic weaknesses that reduce AI discoverability and machine readability.

---

# 🎯 Why AI Readiness Matters

AI-powered systems rely heavily on:

- semantic structure
- metadata quality
- crawlability
- structured content
- schema markup
- FAQs
- semantic hierarchy

Websites optimized for AI systems may gain:

- stronger visibility in AI-generated answers
- improved semantic indexing
- better discoverability in AI search systems
- enhanced machine readability

---

# 🔍 What The Audit Checks

The AI Readiness Audit evaluates:

- Metadata Quality
- Semantic Structure
- Heading Hierarchy
- AI Extractability
- Crawlability
- Schema Markup
- FAQ Presence
- Content Organization
- Semantic SEO Readiness
- Internal Linking
